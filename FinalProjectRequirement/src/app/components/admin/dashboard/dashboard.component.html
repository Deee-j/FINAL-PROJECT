📚 Resources for Quality Education (SDG 4)

Empowering learners, educators, and communities through accessible and inclusive educational tools.

🎓 Educational Platforms

Khan Academy – Free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere.
Coursera – Access university-level courses from top institutions.
UNESCO’s Global Education Coalition – Tools and support for remote learning.

📘 Teaching & Learning Materials

UNICEF Education Toolkit – Resources for teachers, students, and parents in crisis or underdeveloped settings.
TeachSDGs – Curriculum-aligned content for educators integrating global goals.
Open Educational Resources (OER) – Free and openly licensed learning materials.

🌍 Policy & Research

UN SDG 4 Knowledge Hub – Insights, reports, and progress tracking on global education.
Global Education Monitoring (GEM) Report – Annual UNESCO analysis on education systems worldwide.
World Bank Education Data – Statistics and analytics on education access and equity.

💡 Get Involved

Volunteer to Teach – Join global programs to tutor underserved communities.
Sponsor a Student – Support charities that fund school access for disadvantaged youth.
Start a Community Learning Space – Build inclusive local hubs for digital and traditional learning.
